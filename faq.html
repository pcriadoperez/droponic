Frequently Asked Questions

Let us answer all your questions

What technology is behind Droponic?

Droponic uses hydroponic technology, which is a method of growing plants in water without soil. With our hydroponic technology, roots are suspended in water so your plants can easily uptake its vital nutrients with ease. Our intuitive smartphone app will send you reminders to add water and nutrients as necessary, providing a safe and nourishing ecosystem for your plants to flourish. By using hydroponic technology, we guarantee that our product is 100% natural, soil-free, and pesticide-free.

How easy is growing herbs with Droponic?

Droponic makes growing a plant one of the easiest things in the world. Just assemble your planter, add water and nutrients, and watch your plant blossom. Our integrated smart system will always send you a notification when your plant needs nutrients, water, or more sunlight ensuring that your plants are always provided with the optimal conditions to flourish.

Can I use my own seeds?

Our team has conducted extensive research to perfect the hydroponic technology within Droponic. We take great care to source the healthiest seeds and provide the most optimal medium for those seeds to thrive through our carefully crafted coconut-husk capsules. This along with our smart and integrative system will always provide the most ideal ecosystem for your plants to fully thrive. That’s why we always recommend growers to use our seed capsules.

How long does it take for each herb to grow?

Plants grown with Droponic’s hydroponic technology will tend to grow faster than plants grown in soil because nutrients are delivered directly to their roots. With that said, every herb has its own personality and a different grow cycle. It will usually take between two to four weeks to grow your herb with Droponic.

What phones and devices will work with Droponic?

Droponic’s integrative smart system works with both iOS and Android. We are currently developing our system to work with Apple Watch and others smart watches to deliver a fully interconnected growing environment for our growers.

How often do I need to fill Droponic with nutrients and water?

Every herb has its own personality and needs. Growers will usually need to add nutrients and water every two weeks. Droponic’s smart system will always be sure to send a reminder to your smartphone to let you know whenever your plant friend needs a little TLC. With Droponic, keeping your green friend alive has never been easier.

How long can I keep my herbs?

When provided with the right care, your herbs can flourish for months. Our integrated smart system will always send you a notification when your plant needs nutrients, water, or more sunlight. With Droponic’s smart and integrative system, your plants are always provided with the optimal conditions to flourish.

Should I purchase more than one Droponic?

We encourage our growers to have as many Droponics as they please. Spreading many Droponic units throughout your home can spruce the space with fresh and fragrant greens, while allowing for a broader variety of herbs for firing up your favorite recipes in the kitchen.

What plants can I grow?

Our team takes great care to source the healthiest seeds and provide the most optimal medium for those seeds to thrive through our carefully crafted coconut-husk capsules. Right now Droponic can grow: kale, thyme, lavender, basil, parsley, sage, chives, coriander, dill, mint, oregano, and chamomile. We are currently developing our system and adding to our extensive list so our growers can grow a broader variety of greens in the near future.

What are Droponic’s dimensions and weight?

The weight of Droponic with water in it is around 2 pounds. Dimensions: 4” high, 15” long and 5.5” wide

Do you ship to all the world?

Yes, Droponic can be shipped all around the world. Please note that the cost of shipping will be different for each country.

Does Droponic use energy?

With Droponic, your plants will only need water, nutrients, and sunlight to grow. We recommend growers place their plants by the window where they can achieve optimal conditions for absorbing sunlight. Our integrated smart system will always send you a notification when your plant needs nutrients, water, or more sunlight. With energy from the sun, Droponic is able to save up to 70% more water than traditional growth methods and also provide the tastiest herbs.

Are Droponic herbs organic?

Although the hydroponic technology in Droponic grows faster and better tasting greens than those grown in soil, the FDA only provides organic certification to those plants that are grown in soil. Even though Droponic plants are not labeled as organic, we guarantee that our product is 100% natural, soil-free, and pesticide-free.

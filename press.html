Droponic: Growing Your Own Food Has Never Been Easier!

Droponic is proud to announce a revolutionary system for the average consumer to grow food right at home. Beautiful, smart, and elegant, Droponic incorporates techniques from traditional hydroponic fundamentals with a touch of modernism.

Through our “smart” planters paired with our intuitive smartphone app, users can track their plants’ progress, receive reminders to water and add nutrients, and perform light tests to provide the optimum requirements for your plants to flourish. Growing your very own indoor garden has never been easier.

Fire up your favorite dishes with fresh herbs grown directly from your kitchen, without the dirt and grime from traditional planters! We put a great amount of effort in forging the best design for Droponic that is both simple and practical. Droponic attaches effortless to your window, which provides sunlight and energy for its plants. Connected to your smartphone, the system shoots out notifications based on the requirements of individual plants.

“Few things will step up your cooking quite like having fresh herbs to hand. The effect this has on cooking can be huge; before you know it, you’ll find yourself experimenting with flavours with increasing success.” –Jamie Oliver, Top Chef.

Grow anything from kale to basil with ease. We carefully select the best variety vegetable and herbs. Take your cooking game to a whole new level with Droponic!

About Droponic

Droponic started at the hackaton of Draper University Spring 2015 Session. Our first working prototype was created in 48 hours and we had great results. We used a plunger to paste it in a normal window so it was space saving. Then we drilled 4 holes into the stick to insert threading screwing lights bulbs with dried seeds and nutrients inside. The idea was to put a small pump inside the sucker and pump water through the stick so the herbs start to grow with no soil needed.
